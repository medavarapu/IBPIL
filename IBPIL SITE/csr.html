Technical Education

IBEUL has executed MoU with Govt. of Odisha on dtd. 24.05.2010 for establishment of one ITI near our plant area. For this venture, the company has taken a land of 3.20 acres at its own cost without waiting for Govt. land. We have supplemented the giant efforts of the Hon’ble CM in establishing ITI in the trades of Electrician, Fitter & Welder initially at Kumbharbandh in Lakhanpur Tahasil of Jharsuguda District for providing vocational training to the local youth free of cost. The classes have been started from Sept. 2012 with 56 students. The deprived youths of the locality are being given skilled training to enable them to work in Industries. The total expenditure for setting up of this Institution is about Rs. 3.25 crores. Prior to opening of the ITI we have provided multi skilled training to 76 numbers of local unemployed youth through Tata Refractories Limited.

R & R Colony

The Company has set up one R & R Colony for rehabilitation of 8 hutments by constructing quarters to accommodate 22 nos. of displaced families at Mundapada village by following the guideline under R & R policy, 2006 of Govt. of Odisha with all modern amenities. Here we have taken the family criterion as 18 years and above in providing separate households.

Dispensary

Health is wealth. Healthy people are the treasure of any Nation. They are the wealth generator, family makers and good worker. Keeping this in view we have set up one dispensary at plant site. Two MBBS doctors along with supporting staff have been appointed in the dispensary. Free Ambulance service & free medicines are provided to the people around the project area. Company has been creating health awareness among the local people by organizing campaigns for treatment of dengue / blood donation camps very successfully.
