IND-BARATH POWER INFRA LIMITED

Use a spacebar or arrow keys to navigate

  • 3,577.75 MW portfolio
    • 712.75 MW operational (489 MW thermal, 149.5 MW gas, 64 MW biomass, 9.5 MW hydro, 0.75 MW wind)
    • 700 MW to be commissioned by Jun-14 and 810 MW under construction
    • 1,320 MW thermal capacity was under development
    • Expansion of 35 MW gas capacity was under development
  • Robust business model
    • Superior economics as the bulk of the portfolio is located in high power deficit micro markets
    • Generation is sold as a combination of short term and long term power contracts, allowing the company to
      pass on variations in input costs
  • Secured fuel supply
    • Domestic coal linkage for 700 MW of IBEUL (3.07 MTPA linkage) and Phase I (660 MW) of IBEML (1.98 MTPA linkage)
    • Captive coal mines (~32 MTPA) in Indonesia - sufficient for requirements of existing plants
    • Gas linkage for 0.77 scmd available from GAIL
