  • Arkay Energy Rameswarm Limited (AERL)


    AERL, commissioned a 132 MW natural gas-based power project at Ramnathpuram, Tamil Nadu. Further 26.2 MW expansion under progress.

    The estimated fuel requirement is approximately 0.7 million standard metric cubic metres per day at 100.0% PLF and an assumed gross calorific value of 8,830 kcal. AERL has signed an agreement with GAIL (India) Limited (“GAIL”) for supply of natural gas up to 0.292 MMSCMD under APM. We also have access to an additional 0.078+0.40 MMSCMD of natural gas pursuant to a binding term sheet signed with GAIL at MDP.

    Wartsila India Limited was appointed as the O&M contractor and is valid until March 13, 2015.

    AERL delivers the power generated to TNEB Mandapam sub-station using a 110 KV transmission line.

    The plant is located on approximately 12.58 acres of land.

    AERL maintains industrial all risk policies for the plant.

  • Arkay Energy Rameswarm Limited - Expansion (AERL)


    This project is proposed to be undertaken within the existing plant location of the AERL Power Project in Ramnathpuram, Tamil Nadu. This project is expected to be a natural gas-based power project with the capacity to generate up to 26.2 MW of power.

    The main generating equipment will consists of three gas engine generator sets manufactured by a European generator manufacturer, with a capacity of 8.73 MW each.
