About Us

IND-BARATH POWER INFRA LIMITED (IBPIL) is a successful power infrastructure development company with main focus on establishment of power projects of different capacities and utilizing different fuels spread over the country. IBPIL as the holding company provides equity support to its SPV subsidiaries for development of power projects which are again of different categories such as IPPs supplying power to State Utilities, Group captive, merchant plant and hybrid of all. Under the able leadership of Mr. K Raghu, a young and dynamic industrialist.IBPIL is poised for phenomenal growth in the coming years.
