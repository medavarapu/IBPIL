Ind-Barath Energies Thoothukkudi Limited (IBETL)


IBETL commissioned a 20 MW biomass-based power plant at Thoothukudi, Tamil Nadu in June 2006.

The main generating equipment consists of one steam turbine generator set and one boiler manufactured by Hangzhou Steam Turbine Company Limited and Walchandnagar Industries Limited, respectively. The turbine generator set is capable of producing power up to 20 MW.

The estimated fuel requirement is 540 metric tonnes of wood-based biomass per day at 100.0% PLF and an assumed gross calorific value of 2,950 kcal. The primary source of fuel is biomass such as Prosopis Juliflora shrubs combined with wood powder and matchbox waste. We procure our biomass in the surrounding areas.

The plant is operated and maintained by in-house team.

IBETL delivers the power generated using a 110 KV transmission line to the TNEB Eppudumvendren sub-station.

The plant is located on approximately 30.51 acres of land.

IBETL maintains industrial all risk policies for the IBETL Power Project.

Ind-Barath Energies Maharashtra Limited (IBEML)


IBEML, commissioned a 20.0 MW biomass-based power project at Nanded, Maharashtra in January 2009.

The main generating equipment consists of one steam turbine generator set and one boiler manufactured by Hangzhou Steam Turbine Company Limited and Walchandnagar Industries Limited, respectively. The turbine generator set is capable of producing power up to 20 MW.

The estimated fuel requirement is 560 metric tonnes per day of agricultural waste at 100.0% PLF and an assumed gross calorific value of 2,750 kcal. We procure biomass from surrounding areas.

The plant is operated and maintained by in-house team.

IBEML delivers power generated using a 132 KV transmission line to the MSETCL Mukhed sub-station.

The plant is located on approximately 29.58 acres of land.

IBEML maintains a standard fire and special perils policy.

The primary source of fuel is biomass such as Prosopis Juliflora shrubs combined with wood powder and matchbox waste. We procure our biomass from the surrounding areas.

The plant is operated and maintained by in-house team.

RRREL delivers power generated using a 110/230 KV transmission line to the TNEB Melakavadur sub-station.

The plant is located on approximately 21.39 acres of land.

RRREL maintains a standard fire and special perils policy for the RRREL Power Project.

Ind-Barath Energies Limited (IBEL)


IBEL commissioned a 6 MW biomass-based power plant at Nalgonda, Andhra Pradesh in October 2000.

The main generating equipment consists of one steam turbine generator set and one boiler. The turbine and generator are manufactured by a European company and Bharat Heavy Electricals Limited, respectively. The boiler is manufactured by Walchandnagar Industries Limited. The turbine generator set is capable of producing power up to 6.0 MW.

The estimated fuel requirement is 185 metric tonnes per day of biomass rice husk at 100.0% PLF and an assumed gross calorific value of 3,000 kcal. The primary source of fuel is biomass such as rice husk. We procure rice husk from surrounding areas.

The plant is operated and maintained by in-house team.

IBEL delivers power generated using a 33 KV transmission line to the APTRANSCO Miryalaguda sub-station.

The plant is located on approximately 7.47 acres of land owned by IBEL.

IBEL maintains industrial all risk policies for the IBEL Power Project..

Dharmshala Hydro Power Limited (DHPL)


DHPL commissioned 4.5 MW & 5 MW hydroelectric power projects at Kangra, Himachal Pradesh.

The main generating equipment consists of four turbine generator sets manufactured by VA TECH Hydro of Austria. First two turbine generator sets are capable of producing 4.5 MW (2X 2.25 MW) of power and two more turbine generator sets are capable of producing 5 MW (2X2.5MW).

The plant is operated and maintained by in-house team.

DHPL delivers power generated using a 33 KV transmission line to the HPSEB Sidhpur sub-station.

The Maujhi Phase I and II projects are located on approximately 17.04 acres of land. This land was forest land and was granted to DHPL by the Ministry of Environment and Forests. In exchange for the grant, we are required to complete forestation of 35.88 acres of forest land.

DHPL maintains a standard fire and special perils policy.

Ind-Barath Power Infra Limited (IBPIL)


IBPIL commissioned a 0.75 MW wind-based power project at Idukki, Kerala in April 2008.

The main generating equipment of the power project consists of one wind turbine generator set manufactured by Vestas Wind Technology Private Limited. The wind turbine generator set is capable of producing power up to 0.75 MW.

We appointed Vestas Wind Technology Private Limited as the O&M contractor for our wind turbine generator.

The wind-based power plant delivers all of the power generated from this project using a 66 KV transmission line to the KSEB Nedumkandom sub-station.

The wind-based power plant is located on approximately 1.87 acres of land.

We maintain a standard fire and special perils policy for our wind-based power project.
